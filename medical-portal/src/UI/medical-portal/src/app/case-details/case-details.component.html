When would a medical professional submit further information to RoadSafetyBC regarding this patient?
  • A medical professional may be contacted by a patient requesting further medical information that RSBC requires to assess their case.
  • A medical professional can submit further information regarding a patient at any time. This information may include:
    • An amendment* and/or supplement to this DMER submission (e.g. relevant specialist consult reports, results of medical investigations, etc.)
    • Any other information they believe should be taken into consideration when assessing this patient's fitness to drive.
