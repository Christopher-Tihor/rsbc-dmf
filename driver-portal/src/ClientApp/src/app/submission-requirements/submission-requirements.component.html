RSBC is asking for specific submissions to conduct their evaluation. These submissions can be documents or reports that you or your medical practitioner can provide. Once you upload a document or report, the requirement will be moved to the Document History tab.


Please be aware that if your medical practitioner or you have sent a document by fax, it may take some time for it to be removed from this list.
